A project I built to turn a plain search — a business category and a city — into a scored, enriched prospect list. It queries Google Maps, scores each business's digital presence, detects the marketing pixels they run (Meta, Google, TikTok and more) and collects their reviews, then delivers a ready-to-use spreadsheet by email. Digital Presence Score

A transparent 0–100 heuristic weighs website, contact completeness, rating and review volume.

websitecontactabilityratingvolume
0 — 40 · hot lead
Little online presence — prime candidate for digital marketing services.
71 — 100 · benchmark
Digitally strong — a useful reference point or a competitor to monitor.

The lower the score, the bigger the opportunity — the scoring is what turns a raw scrape into a prioritized list.

Run the pipeline yourself

This is the actual pipeline described above, not a mockup. Enter a category and a city, and it runs the five stages live against Google Maps and emails you the resulting spreadsheet — scores, pixels and reviews included.

  • Any category + city (e.g. dental clinics miami)
  • Runs in ~5 minutes — hosted on a free-tier server, so give it a moment
  • Check spam if the email doesn't show up
  • Nothing is stored — results go only to the address you enter
⚙ Fixed scope: 5 businesses × 5 reviews — kept small so the free server stays responsive.
Results go only to the email you provide. Nothing is stored.
